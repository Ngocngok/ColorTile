Tutorial Image Placeholders
===========================

Please replace these placeholder images with your actual tutorial images:

1. tutorial_step1.png - First tutorial step
2. tutorial_step2.png - Second tutorial step  
3. tutorial_step3.png - Third tutorial step

Recommended size: 800x800 pixels or similar square/portrait format
Format: PNG with transparency support

After adding your images:
1. Import them into Unity
2. Set Texture Type to "Sprite (2D and UI)"
3. Assign them to the TutorialManager component in the Game scene:
   - tutorialStep1
   - tutorialStep2
   - tutorialStep3
